Matcha Cookie Recipe!

Here is my white chocolate matcha cookie recipe. Its my favourite!

For the ingredients, you’ll need 1/2 cup of butter (melted and browned), 2 eggs, 2 cups of flour, 1 tbsp of vanilla extract, 1 teaspoon of baking powder, 1/4 teaspoon of salt, 1/2 cup of brown sugar, 1/2 cup of white sugar, 1/2 cup of white chocolate chips, and 1 tbsp of matcha powder. To prepare, brown the butter on heat, then mix the dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, salt, and matcha powder) together. After cooling the butter for 3 minutes, add it to the bowl along with the sugars and mix. Add one egg at a time, followed by the vanilla extract, then continue mixing and gradually add the dry ingredients. Fold in the white chocolate chips, then chill the dough for about 2 hours. Once chilled, shape the dough into fist-sized balls, flatten them a little, and bake at 350°F for 11 minutes. Enjoy!
