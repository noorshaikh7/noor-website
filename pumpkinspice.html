Pumpkin Spice Cookie Recipe

Here is my pumpkin spice cookies with cream cheese, perfect for autumn.

For these pumpkin cookies, you'll need 1 cup softened butter, 1 cup granulated sugar, 1 teaspoon baking powder, 1 teaspoon baking soda, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon, 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg, 1 egg, 1 teaspoon vanilla, 1 cup canned pumpkin, and 2 cups all-purpose flour. For the cream cheese frosting, you'll need 1/2 cup softened butter, 8 oz softened cream cheese, 4 cups powdered sugar, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F and line a cookie sheet with parchment paper. Beat together butter, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg until combined. Add the egg, vanilla, and pumpkin, then mix in the flour. Drop dough by heaping teaspoons onto the sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes until set. For the frosting, beat together butter and cream cheese, then mix in powdered sugar and vanilla until smooth. Frost cooled cookies and enjoy!
